Oh, hai.  irccat is like `cat`, but here, the STDOUT is an IRC channel.

/!\ The project is active again - A new release will come soon with a lot of funny things !

The bot receives messages by TCP or HTTP and sends the message, without any treatment, to the channel. Simple! (original idea by last.fm)

It's free and opensource software, released under the MIT license.

why?

With irccat, you can send: SVN or Git commits, new twitters, new items in RSS, ... and so much more!
